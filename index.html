About Me

Hi there! You are looking into one of the most determined and passionate people you'll ever meet. I was raised by my incredible Grandma to value hard work and to take pride in all I do. I have come a looooong way to get to where I am which is holding a Bachelor's degree in Aerospace Engineering, a Masters Degree in Systems Engineering, a Masters in Business Administration, and most fulfilling being a mother of two beautiful children. I owe all the good in me and my success to my amazing Grandma.

I had the determination, and she showed me the way. I wanted more than life could ever grant me. Today, I am proud to say is the greatest, still I'm always looking to improve on myself and get further ahead. I can't wait for tomorrow and all it's possibilities.
